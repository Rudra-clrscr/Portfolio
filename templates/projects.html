EDA On Netflix Dataset

Exploratory Data Analysis of Netflix’s Movie & TV Show Dataset! 📈

As a data enthusiast, I dove deep into this dataset using Python (pandas, numpy, seaborn, matplotlib) to uncover fascinating trends and insights.
Here’s what I did:
✅ Cleaned and preprocessed the data, tackling missing values and inconsistencies.
✅ Analyzed patterns in genres, release years, and ratings to understand content trends.
✅ Created engaging visualizations to highlight key findings, like the rise of specific genres or shifts in content production over time.
This project was a great opportunity to sharpen my data analysis and visualization skills while exploring a dataset tied to an industry I love!

EDA On IPL Dataset

Exploratory Data Analysis of Indian Primier League 🏏📊

In this project, I applied Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) to clean and analyze 10 years of IPL match data. I explored trends in team performances, venue statistics, match outcomes, and victory patterns, presenting insights through clear and impactful visualizations. This project strengthened my skills in:
✅ Data cleaning & preprocessing
✅ Statistical analysis
✅ Data visualization & storytelling
✅ Turning raw data into actionable insights
I’m excited to bring these skills into real-world data projects and continue growing in the field of Data Analytics & Data Science.
